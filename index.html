Our Goal

The goal of Location Lockdown is to show that while the COVID-19 pandemic has been uncertain beyond belief, the world has been taking time during this lockdown period to appreciate the little things in life. By curating a collection of moments spanning all of the continents, we hope to bring a smile to your face, while also supporting the valued and critical work of the World Health Organisation, who are fighting on the front line every day.
